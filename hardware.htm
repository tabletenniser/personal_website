BACKGROUND

    This section provides a brief summary on my hardware development background ranging from analog circuits at the physical level to basic computer architectures.

SKILL SUMMARY

Analog Circuits

-Relevant in-lab experience working with different electrical elements on protoboards.
-Use Kirchoff's Law to analyze linear circuits
-Utilize concepts of phasers and impedance to solve time-dependent circuits
-Knowledge about physical characteristics and behavior of Op-amp, diodes, BJT and MOSFET transistors

Logical Circuits

-Familiar with different types of logical gates (e.g AND, OR, XOR etc.) and how they are built from transistors.
-Analyze and design simple logical circuits using finite state machine.
-Timing analysis for circuits containing flip-flops and registers.
-In-lab experience using Verilog as the hardware descriptive language to design and implement hardware circuits on Altera FPGA chips
Computer Organization

-Familiar with modern micro-processor structure and how it communicates with memory and different devices.
-In-lab assembly programming experience on Altera NiosII processor.
-Knowledge of memory hierarchy including structures of different types of cache memory.
